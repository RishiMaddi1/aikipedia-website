🧠 About Aikipedia

Aikipedia is on a mission to make the world's most advanced AI tools accessible, affordable, and available to everyone.
We believe AI should be a tool for empowerment, not a luxury behind paywalls or international cards.

Whether you're a student exploring creativity, a developer building smarter tools, or just curious about the power of conversational AI — Aikipedia gives you instant access to the latest AI models at transparent, pay-as-you-go pricing.

Built with a modern, scalable architecture leveraging Cloudflare Workers, Supabase Edge Functions, and robust cloud infrastructure for reliability and speed.

🌟 What Makes Aikipedia Unique?

  • Access the Best AI Models: From natural conversations to powerful coding assistants and research bots, we bring you state-of-the-art AI tools under one roof, delivered via globally distributed edge functions for low-latency access.

  • No Subscriptions, No Hassles: Say goodbye to expensive monthly fees. Just buy credits once, and use them anytime. No commitments, no auto-renewals.

  • Pay in Indian Rupees via UPI: We've made AI truly accessible in India — pay securely using UPI apps like GPay, PhonePe, or Paytm, starting from as low as ₹49.

  • Realtime Usage, Realtime Value: Credits (Echos) are added instantly after payment, letting you use the platform without delays or waiting for manual approvals. Our backend leverages serverless functions and scalable databases for seamless experience.

  • For Users and Developers Alike: Use our sleek chat interface or integrate AI models into your apps through API keys linked to your account. Track usage, manage costs, and stay in control, all powered by a secure, cloud-native stack.
